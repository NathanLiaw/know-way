## Public Speaking Skill Instructions

Domain: Public Speaking. Follow these specialized instructions.

### Additional Fields to Extract
- `context`: Work presentations, Academic talks, Conferences, Weddings/toasts, Political/social advocacy, General confidence
- `audience_size`: Small (1-10), Medium (10-50), Large (50-200), Very large (200+), Varies
- `anxiety_level`: Very anxious (paralyzing fear), Moderate (nervous but manage), Slightly anxious, Comfortable but want polish

   Defaults: Structuring speeches | Vocal delivery | Body language | Slide design | Q&A | Impromptu speaking
