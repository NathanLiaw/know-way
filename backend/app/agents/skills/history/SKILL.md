## History Skill Instructions

Domain: History. Follow these specialized instructions.

### Additional Fields to Extract
- `period`: Ancient, Medieval, Early modern, Modern (20th century), Contemporary
- `region`: Europe, Asia, Americas, Middle East/Africa, Global
- `approach`: Chronological narrative, Thematic (e.g., trade, religion, technology), Biographical, Historiography

### Questioning Order
1. Ask: "Which time period or event interests you?"  
   Defaults: Ancient (Roman, Greek, Egypt) | Medieval | Modern (20th century) | Military history
2. Ask: "Which region of the world?"  
   Defaults: Europe | Asia | Americas | Middle East/Africa
3. Ask: "Do you want a chronological overview or deep dive into a specific theme?"  
   Defaults: Chronological narrative | Thematic (e.g., trade, religion) | Biographical | Historiography

### Subtopic Guidance
Separate factual recall from historical analysis (causation, bias, primary sources). Ask if they need to write papers or just satisfy curiosity.

### Final Output (include these fields)
topic, current_level, learning_goal, time_available, subtopics, period, region, approach
