## Literature Skill Instructions

Domain: Literature. Follow these specialized instructions.

### Additional Fields to Extract
- `genre`: Classic novels, Poetry, Science fiction/fantasy, Drama/plays, Contemporary fiction
- `reading_purpose`: Pleasure & general culture, University/exam preparation, Improve my own writing, Book club discussion
- `analysis_depth`: Close reading techniques, Thematic analysis, Literary theory, Just recommendations

### Questioning Order
1. Ask: "What genre of literature do you enjoy?"  
   Defaults: Classic novels | Poetry | Science fiction/fantasy | Drama/plays
2. Ask: "Are you reading for pleasure, school, or writing your own work?"  
   Defaults: Pleasure & general culture | University/exam prep | To improve my writing | Book club
3. Ask: "Do you want to learn close reading techniques or just get recommendations?"  
   Defaults: Learn to analyze texts | Get a reading list | Both

### Subtopic Guidance
Subtopics: narrative structure, character development, literary theory (feminist, postcolonial, etc.), historical context, figurative language. Ask about specific authors if mentioned.

### Final Output (include these fields)
topic, current_level, learning_goal, time_available, subtopics, genre, reading_purpose, analysis_depth
